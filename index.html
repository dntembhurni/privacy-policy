Bloomlabz Pvt Ltd

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 9, 2026
App: Number Fun

This Privacy Policy explains how Bloomlabz Pvt Ltd handles information in Number Fun. Number Fun is designed as a child-directed number learning app with local-only progress, no accounts, no advertising, no analytics, no tracking, and no social features. Number Fun does not transmit user data off the device.

Information stored on this device

Number Fun saves basic learning progress on the device so parents can review practice history and the app can continue from where the child left off. This progress is processed only inside the app on the device and is not sent to Bloomlabz Pvt Ltd or another company.

Local progress may include app activity such as completed number practice, current learning position, and parent-controlled settings. Number Fun uses this local information only to provide app functionality, show progress to parents, and let the app continue from the last saved point.

Google Play Data Safety

For Google Play Data Safety purposes, Number Fun does not collect or share user data. The app does not transmit local progress, child activity, device identifiers, diagnostics, crash logs, or other user data to Bloomlabz Pvt Ltd or to third parties.

Number Fun does not include third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, social, account, purchase, subscription, or crash-reporting SDKs. If a future version changes these practices, Bloomlabz Pvt Ltd will update this policy and the Google Play Data Safety declaration before release.

Information we do not collect

Number Fun does not ask for or collect a child name, parent name, email address, phone number, photos, videos, voice recordings, precise or approximate location, contacts, calendar data, payment information, advertising ID, Android ID, or open-ended child content. The app does not create profiles or measure behavior for analytics or advertising.

Number Fun does not collect authentication information, account credentials, health information, financial information, messages, app lists, browser history, search history, or information from other apps on the device.

No ads, sale, or sharing

Number Fun does not show ads, sponsored content, rewarded ads, subscriptions, paid upgrades, or in-app purchases. Bloomlabz Pvt Ltd does not sell, rent, or share child data with advertisers, data brokers, analytics providers, social networks, or any other third party.

Permissions and device features

Number Fun does not request access to the camera, microphone, contacts, photos, videos, files, phone, SMS, calendar, precise location, approximate location, nearby devices, or notifications. The app does not transfer user data to another app on the device.

Device text to speech

Number Fun uses the device text-to-speech feature to speak numbers and short learning prompts. Number Fun does not record the child, use the microphone, or upload speech audio.

Security and data handling

Because Number Fun does not transmit personal or sensitive user data, there is no app-to-server transfer of user data to secure. Local progress remains within the app storage area managed by the device operating system.

Parents should avoid sending unnecessary child personal information when contacting Bloomlabz Pvt Ltd about the app.

Retention, deletion, and backups

Local progress remains on the device until the app is deleted or a parent uses Reset Progress in Parent View. Reset Progress deletes the saved learning progress from Number Fun on that device. Because Number Fun does not create accounts, there is no app account to delete and no server-side app data deletion request is needed.

Device backups, restores, family-sharing settings, and device transfer tools are controlled by the parent or device owner outside Number Fun.

Children and parent control

Number Fun is intended for use by children with parent or guardian supervision. Parent View is behind a long press and includes progress review, reset controls, this Privacy Policy, Terms and Conditions, Data & Child Safety information, and required notices.

Changes and contact

Bloomlabz Pvt Ltd may update this policy when Number Fun changes. Parents can submit privacy questions, support requests, or other app-related inquiries through the Bloomlabz Pvt Ltd developer contact details provided on the Google Play store listing or by emailing smita@bloomlabz.com.

Email sent to smita@bloomlabz.com is handled by Smita, Director and Founder of Bloomlabz Pvt Ltd. If a parent chooses to contact Bloomlabz Pvt Ltd, any contact details or message content the parent provides are used only to respond to that request and are not linked to the child's local app progress.
