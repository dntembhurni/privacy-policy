Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 9, 2026
App: Number Fun

This Privacy Policy explains how Bloomlabz Pvt Ltd handles information in Number Fun. Number Fun is designed as a child-directed number learning app with local-only progress, no accounts, no advertising, no analytics, no tracking, and no social features.

Information stored on this device

Number Fun saves basic learning progress on the device so parents can review practice history and the app can continue from where the child left off. This progress stays inside the app on the device and is not sent to Bloomlabz Pvt Ltd or another company.

Information we do not collect

Number Fun does not ask for or collect a child name, parent name, email address, phone number, photos, videos, voice recordings, location, contacts, calendar data, payment information, or open-ended child content. The app does not create profiles or measure behavior for analytics or advertising.

No ads, sale, or sharing

Number Fun does not show ads, sponsored content, rewarded ads, subscriptions, paid upgrades, or in-app purchases. Bloomlabz Pvt Ltd does not sell, rent, or share child data with advertisers, data brokers, analytics providers, or social networks.

Device text to speech

Number Fun uses the device text-to-speech feature to speak numbers and short learning prompts. Number Fun does not record the child, use the microphone, or upload speech audio.

Retention, deletion, and backups

Local progress remains on the device until the app is deleted or a parent uses Reset Progress in Parent View. Reset Progress deletes the saved learning progress from Number Fun on that device. Device backups, restores, family-sharing settings, and device transfer tools are controlled by the parent or device owner outside Number Fun.

Children and parent control

Number Fun is intended for use by children with parent or guardian supervision. Parent View is behind a long press and includes progress review, reset controls, this Privacy Policy, Terms and Conditions, Data & Child Safety information, and required notices.

Changes and contact

Bloomlabz Pvt Ltd may update this policy when Number Fun changes. Parents can contact Bloomlabz Pvt Ltd through the developer contact details provided on the app store listing.
